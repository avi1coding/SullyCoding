About the Creator

Hi! I'm Avi Mehta, and I created AMported because I wanted to give students access to fun, unblocked games during their free time. I noticed that many gaming sites were blocked at school, and students didn't have a reliable place to play games during breaks or study hall.

This isn't a school project or assignment - it's something I built on my own time because I believe students deserve a way to relax and have fun during their downtime. Gaming can be a great stress reliever and a fun way to take a break from studying.

I taught myself web development to create AMported. The site is built using modern HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to ensure it loads quickly and works smoothly on any device.
