Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will my child see results? +
Most kids build their first working game within the first hour of starting the course. By week one, they'll have created multiple games and gained confidence in their coding abilities. The course is designed for quick wins to keep them motivated.
What if my child has no coding experience? +
Perfect! This course is specifically designed for complete beginners. We start with the absolute basics and guide them step-by-step. No prior experience required - just curiosity and willingness to learn.
Do you offer refunds if it's not a good fit? +
Absolutely! We offer a 7-day free trial and a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll refund your purchase with no questions asked.
How long does the course take to complete? +
The core course includes 5+ hours of video content, but most students spend 2-3 weeks working through it at their own pace. The beauty is they can go as fast or slow as they want, and they get lifetime access to revisit any lessons.
What devices does the course work on? +
The course works on any device with internet access - computers, tablets, even smart TVs. Scratch runs in any web browser, so there's nothing to download or install. Works on Windows, Mac, iPad, Android, and Chromebooks.
Do you offer group discounts for multiple children? +
Yes! We offer special pricing for families with multiple children, homeschool co-ops, and schools. Contact us with your specific needs and we'll create a custom package that works for your budget.
